Earth Force

The earth element is the first and perhaps the most important element to study. In this element the practitioners learn how to grounding and centering in the real self, standing like an unmovable mountain.

The training of Earth element is focus on body structure developing (muscle, joint and tendon), relaxing, Hara centering, grounding and general self-defense.
The training includes:

Body movement: rolling and break fall, forward, backwards & sideways
Attack: striking, kicking and punching
Defense: grappling, throwing, choking, and joint locks
Mind science: mudra and meditations for Earth Force.
Also the practitioner to pass to the water level must learn the practice of Iron Shirt 1, and Tai Chi Qi Gong 1
